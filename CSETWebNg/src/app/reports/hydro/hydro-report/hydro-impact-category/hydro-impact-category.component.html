Impact Category

Impact category is the classification of where a particular impact may happen if a cyber attack were to occur. The breakdown of the chart is by domain, with different categories that we have defined as most applicable to this assessment: Economic, Environmental, Operational, and Safety. {{facilityName}} can see the breakdown of how the answers of the assessment relate to what potential impacts the facility may have in case of a cyberattack.
